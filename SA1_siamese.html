Siamese Cat

Siamese Cat from its name originated from Thailand with modern variety distinct features almond-shaped blue eyes, large ears, triangular shaped head, and long and slender body.

Their breed is known to be extremely lively and affectionate to humans. In addition, they are very intelligent and curious to their surroundings.
