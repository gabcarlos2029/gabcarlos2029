Persian Cat

Persian Cat, aka Persian Longhair is a popular breed, and it is adored by many because of its cuteness, long haired, petite nose, and flat nose, which is a rare variety.

These cats originated from its namesake, Persia (Iran), and they are highly valued because of their beauty and docile personality.
