Maine Coon

Maine Coon is a natural breed that originates from North America, common in the state of Maine in the United States.

It is a large cat with a dog-like personality, so it is also used for hunting.

An adult cat can grow as much as 10-16 inches in length. It can also reach up to 15 kg in weight.

Fun Fact: Maine Coon is the largest domestic cat breed in the whole world!
